This photo is of Rafael Marti aboard Pez Piedra fishing in the White Marlin Tournament in Cap Cana circa 2018.

This week's Fisherman Spotlight goes to Rafael Marti has been fishing for about 12 years now and has won numerous tournaments as captain, fisherman, team and boat owner. He boat is the Pez Piedra, whom has a record of being one of the fastes fisherman boats in Puerto Rico and Dominican Republic and has records in tournaments qualifying the boat and it's crew to many invitational tournaments.
